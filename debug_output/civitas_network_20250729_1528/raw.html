Doing it alone is hard.
Working together
makes it easier.

There’ve been many studies on high-growth companies and they all say the same thing:

Members of CEO networks are significantly more likely to build large companies.

That’s because, whatever your origins and whatever your business’s size, it’s tough to build something alone – company, brand, product or service. While you’re not necessarily short on pats on the back, it can be hard to stay motivated; to make big decisions; to level up. But when you add the support of founders just like you, your challenges are shared and your opportunities are multiplied.

Easier said than done? Actually, no. At Civitas we see it happen daily.

What is Civitas?

Civitas is a private network for high-growth founders. We bring together the top founders and CEOs in each city to connect and learn from each other. Our offering includes a digital community with online mentoring sessions, chat groups, meetups, and access to a CEO playbook - one page solutions to common challenges faced by CEOs.

We also offer an in-person community with regular events, from intimate dinners with high-profile CEOs to unique experiences with fellow founders. You’ll also be part of a monthly CEO forum with a carefully curated group of 7 founders to share learnings, overcome challenges, and build lasting friendships.

Do I qualify?

Civitas is for founders or CEOs of high-growth businesses. You must be the founder, owner, or person in charge of all operations (CEO or MD). Your business must be generating at least R5 million ($250k) in annual revenue. We encourage rare and exceptional founders to apply regardless of revenue.

Our members lead businesses between R5 million ($250k) and R300 million ($15 million), with teams ranging from 5 to 2000 team members. We seek growth-focused, generous, collaborative members with a pay-it-forward attitude. 
‍

What is the time commitment?

There’s no fixed time commitment. Some members get their value from the digital community, CEO Playbook, and WhatsApp groups, while others attend a few of our 10 to 15 monthly online and in-person events. If you decide to join a CEO forum, you’ll be required to commit to just 2-hours each month. 

How does the application process work?

The application process is simple:

  1. Fill out the quick online application which should take 2 minutes to complete
  2. We’ll evaluate it and let you know if you’ve been successful. If you’re a fit, we’ll schedule a 25-minute Zoom to take you through the offering in detail. 
  3. Once onboarded, we’ll ensure you're getting value from the network right away. 
I am fairly well-connected. Do I need this?

Even if you have a large network, many of our members discover the value of a structured, peer-driven learning environment. Through facilitated discussions and fresh ideas, our sessions are tailored to address your unique challenges, uncover blind spots, and provide a consistent space to gain new perspectives.

These incremental shifts compound into significant transformations over time. Plus, members appreciate the opportunity to connect with founders at various stages of growth in a time-efficient and impactful way.
